⚙️ MayaMergev1.1 New release 2026-07-24
MayaMerge — move objects between scenes, like Merge in 3ds Max MayaMerge lets you open any file (.mb/.ma/.fbx/.obj), pick exactly the objects you need from a handy list, and bring only those into your current scene — with no junk: only the selection's own materials, skin and animation come across. Its highlight is Detach mode: you can pull a working sub-rig (say, a weapon with its control bones) out of a character as a standalone, still-animated prop, with animation and skin intact. And for total isolation there's an off-screen mode, where your scene never touches the source file at all. Who it's for: animators, riggers, and anyone who assembles scenes from pieces and is tired of dragging clutter out of other people's files.
