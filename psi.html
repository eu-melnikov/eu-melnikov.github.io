// About

Imagine you have terabytes of photos and videos scattered across multiple drives — external SSDs, old HDDs, NAS storage. Finding a specific shot means plugging in the right drive, waiting for the system to recognise it, and digging through folders.

PSI solves this. It scans your media folders once and builds a local thumbnail cache. After that, you can browse your entire collection — thousands, tens of thousands of files — even when the original drives are completely offline. The thumbnails are already on your machine.

// Features

🖼️
Photos & Video
Supports JPEG, PNG, HEIC, RAW formats, MP4, MOV and more. Video files get an automatic preview from the first frames.
⚡
Offline browsing
Index your folders once — then browse the full thumbnail gallery at any time, even with the source drives disconnected.
🗂️
Three gallery layouts
Waterfall, justified grid, or list view. Filter by orientation and file type. Quick search by filename.
❤️
Favourites & collections
Group photos into collections by project, event, or anything you like. Collections work fully offline too.
📦
Portable cache
Index on your workstation, copy the cache to your laptop — your entire thumbnail library travels with you.
🌐
Two languages
English and Russian interface, switchable in one click. Same cache files work on both.
